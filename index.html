Editing Work

The Scope Boston

As editor-in-chief, I strategize its coverage, delivering on average 5 stories per week, including projects with video, audio and data visualization and interactive elements with a team of 4 part-time student reporters. I helped launched its first news-by-text service delivering essential COVID-19 information to residents relying on text messaging for communication. During a one year span, I grew The Scope's audience by 140%. The Scope is a digital publication reporting stories of social justice, hope and resilience in Boston underserved neighborhoods.

Global Observer

An online magazine I founded in 2018. The magazine's work has been featured on The Boston Globe, WBUR webcasts and CBS Boston. The magazine is now run by a group of graduate students at Northeastern University School of Journalism.
