JRNL 3610 - Tool Review Project Showcase

Sounds of Boston - Podcast
By Riana Buchman

Buchman asks 20 random people on the streets of Boston the question: “What sounds remind you of Boston?” After the interviews, she walked around Boston recording the sounds interviewees mentioned. “Sounds of Boston” provides a unique perspective into the soundscape of the city we live in.

Exploring Boston's religious treasures - 360 Photography
By Abigail Hembree

Hembree creates a series of 360 tours of churches in Boston using the Ricoh Theta V camera to take the photos and Kuula, a digital platform to share the 360 images.

Commercial Ad for 'Shot Quality' - Video
By Matt Levin

Levin uses Adobe Premiere and Photoshop create an ad for social media for the company Shot Qualityhere.

Iran national football team’s profile - Magazine
By Daniel Elahi

Elahi uses Photoshop to create a sports magazine-inspired feature of the Iran national football team’s profile. The feature is divided into three sections: a brief summary of the team’s history, their journey to the 2022 World Cup in Qatar, and their future development. 

Hollywood Mysteries: Natalie Wood - Podcast
By Julia Smith

Smith uses a Yeti Blue USB microphone to record a conversation with her roommate Mia on the mysterious death of Hollywood star Natalie Wood and Adobe Audition to edit and produce the podcast.

Mixed Reality with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Guides
By Michelle Matiash

Matiash uses Microsoft Dynamics 365 Guides to create a digital storytelling project. She built an experience that presents the Coloro and WGSN color projections for 2023 using an immersive storytelling angle. Project can only be seen with a VR headset.

Noises of the Neighborhoods - Soundcite
By Ana Macek

Macek uses Soundcite to add sounds into text in her project which documents background noise in Boston’s various neighborhoods.

Prison Abolition - Graphic Design
By Ali Dusinberre

Dusinberre uses Piktochart to create two infographics about prison abolition.

BPS student and teacher diversity - Data Visualization
By Maya Homan

Homan uses Tableau to visualize a dataset of Boston Public School student and teacher diversity.

Interactive Website
By Julia Carlin

Carlin uses Cargo.site and PremierPro to create a website website that replicates an old computer desktop and allows users to click on various desktop icons that lead to pictures, audio interviews, music, video, and more to tell stories of an artist.

Travel Blog
By Judy Lam

Lam uses Adobe Lightroom to edit photos for her travel blog.

Data Visualization with Mapbox
By Pavithra Rajesh

Rajesh uses Mapbox to create a data visualization.

Data Visualization with Tableau
By Ilana Gersten

Gersten uses Tableau to create a magazine layout.

Data Visualization with Thinglink
By Margot Kohler

Kohler uses Thinglink to visualize Trump's Judicial Appointees to the U.S. Court of Appeals.

Ethical Reporting Magazine
By Quillan Anderson

Anderson uses Adobe InDesign to create a magazine layout.

“Behind the Mask” Podcast
By Sarah Olender

Olender uses Adobe Audition to produce a podcast about the furry community.

Data Visualization with Thinglink
By Zoe Sommers

Sommers uses Adobe Premiere Pro to create a concert video.

Podcast
