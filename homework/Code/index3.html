Early Coronavirus Reactions

The coronavirus outbreak has seem to explode and soon the U.S. may end up with the worst COVID-19 outbreak in the industrialized world. How did a country with supposedly the best medical infrastructure in the world be so ill-prepared for the pandemic when we were warned about it since early January. Let's take a look at the sentiment of President Trump's reaction to the coronavirus and compare that to the reaction of health experts, scientists, the Center for Disease Control and the World Health Organization.
